Frequently Asked Questions

App doesn't play a sound?

This app does not support external playback devices like CarPlay or headphones at this time. To avoid any playback issues, please ensure that all such devices are disconnected from your phone before using the app. We appreciate your understanding as we work on expanding compatibility in future updates.

How can I update the app?

Open the App Store and tap your profile icon to view available updates. Locate the Beet App and tap "Update" to download and install the latest version. Once the update completes, launch the app to enjoy its new features.

Is my data secure?

Our app is designed with your security in mind. It processes audio in real time to create a visual representation and immediately discards the complete audio file, ensuring that no recordings are stored. This approach guarantees that your privacy is always protected.
